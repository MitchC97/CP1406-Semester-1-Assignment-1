Slammers Beach Volleyball Club

Welcome! Slammers Beach is your local Volleyball Club for kids, young adults, Men and Woman of all ages. Families are welcome too! Whether you're brand new, still learning or a seasoned pro, gather up some friends, family members or join a team from the Season page and get slamming! What is Volleyball you ask? See the above link for more information. There's also current season standings, and a page for you to join us Slammers on.
Slammers beach is family-friendly, and has been around for years providing quality courts and sports facilities to young people of all ages, whether you're still in school or have left college. You're never too old to slam, and there's over 800 million people worldwide doing it! It's a safe, fun and low-impact sport suitable for all fitness levels. Whether you're a casual slammer, or a competitive slammer, you're guaranteed to have an intense, exciting experience.
